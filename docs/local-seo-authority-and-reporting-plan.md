## Monthly Search Console Report

Export or record these fields monthly:

- Total clicks, impressions, CTR, and average position.
- Top non-brand queries: sell my house fast spokane, sell my house cda, cash home buyers spokane, we buy houses spokane, cash for homes spokane.
- Top pages by impressions and clicks.
- Pages with impressions and zero clicks.
- Indexed page count.
- Not indexed count by reason.
- Discovered - currently not indexed count.
- Crawled - currently not indexed count.
- External links total and top linked pages.
- Internal links total and whether money pages are gaining links.

Decision rule: if impressions rise but clicks stay flat, revise titles/meta and proof language. If pages stay discovered but not crawled, add internal links from indexed pages and improve uniqueness/usefulness before requesting indexing again.
